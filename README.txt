To Compile:

– Open Assignment 1.xcodeproj in Xcode (Xcode 5 or higher supported)
– Cmd+B will build the application

To Run:

– Open Assignment 1.xcodeproj in Xcode (Xcode 5 or higher supported)
– Click the large friendly play button in the top left corner, or Cmd+R
